
# 💫 About Me:
Hey! I'm Sree Haran (Shady) — a Final year B.Tech. student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Sri Krishna College of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore. Graduated Diploma in Mechatronics at Nachimuthu Polytechnic college, Pollachi.<br><br>🔩 Embedded Systems Side:<br>I'm passionate about low-level systems — bare-metal ARM programming, FreeRTOS on STM32, memory management (MPU/MMU/cache), Proteus, STM32-CubeIDE, Matlab, and building RTOS. My career target is automotive embedded systems with aerospace/defense organizations like Aerospace engineers, TEAL, Tata Advanced Systems, BEL, and L&T — working toward becoming an Automotive Embedded & Edge AI Architect.<br><br>🤖 AI Engineering Side:<br>Currently leveling up in AI Engineering — working with LLMs, RAG pipelines, prompt & context engineering, vector databases, Learning Advance concepts like embeddings, LangChain, LangGraph, HuggingFace Transformers, model distillation, and agent building.
